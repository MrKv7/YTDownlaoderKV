Everything you need to know

Is this service completely free?

Yes — 100% free. No subscriptions, hidden fees, or accounts required. Download as much as you want, forever.

What video qualities can I download?

Any quality that YouTube offers for that specific video — from 144p all the way to 4K Ultra HD, plus audio-only MP3.

Can I download from any YouTube channel?

Yes — any publicly available video from any channel. Private or age-restricted content is not supported.

How long does a download take?

It depends on your internet speed and the file size. Our live progress bar shows real-time speed and ETA.

Is there a download limit?

No hard limits. Download as many videos as you need. Please respect copyright laws and creators' rights.

Which browsers are supported?

All modern browsers — Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari, Opera — on both desktop and mobile devices.
